Doing the Right Thing

GD employees are united by our Ethos—our defining moral character as a company. This Ethos undergirds our culture and our commitment to our communities, employees, customers and shareholders. It drives and determines how we do business and informs our commitment to good corporate citizenship, sustainable business practices and community support.
